OUR WORK

What we've done for people.

MaMome is at the forefront of innovation in the field of pregnancy care with its proprietary testing and bioinformatic pipeline platform, which is poised to revolutionize the way we approach early diagnostics and management of pregnancy complications using microbiome-based techniques.

Complications like preeclampsia, gestational diabetes, and preterm birth cause long-term health effects for both mother and baby and increase your risk for birth interventions. Accurately identifying women at higher risk for pregnancy complications is the first step to preventing pregnancy complications. Help us unlock the maternal microbiome by participating in one of our research studies. We provide all participants with no-cost wellness coaching to assist with a healthy diet and other healthy pregnancy behaviors. Studies are completely non-invasive, and you can participate from your own home.
